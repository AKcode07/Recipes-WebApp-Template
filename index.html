Gulab Jamun


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of milk powder
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 tsp of baking soda
  • 2 tbsp of ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of water
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp of cardamom powder and 1 tsp of rose water (both optional)

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, mix 1 cup of milk powder, 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour, 1/4 tsp of baking soda, and a pinch of cardamom powder.
  2. Add 2 tbsp of ghee and 3-4 tbsp of milk to form a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small, crack-free balls and fry them in hot oil or ghee until they turn deep golden brown (7-10 minutes).
  4. In a saucepan, combine 1 cup of sugar and 1 cup of water. Boil to form a slightly sticky syrup. Add cardamom powder and rose water, then soak the fried gulab jamun in the warm syrup for at least 2-3 hours.

Secret Tip:

Elevate your gulab jamun by infusing saffron into the milk, adding chopped nuts to the dough, and garnishing with extra nuts or edible silver leaf for a delightful and visually appealing twist.
